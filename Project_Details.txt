-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Convert feet, inche into centimeters
(feet * 30.48) + (inche * 2.54) = height (cm)

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Age: 4 to 90 yrs
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Weight: 10 to 200 kg
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Height: 134.62 to 218.44 cm
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Body Mass Index (BMI)

Formula:
BMI = weight(kg) / (height(cm) * 0.01)^2

BMI Categories
Underweight: < 18.5
Normal:	18.5 to 24.9
Overweight: 25 to 29.9
Obesity: > 30
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) (kcal Per Day)
BMR = 88.362 + (13.397 * weight in kg) + (4.799 * height in cm) - (5.677 * age in years) [Men]
BMR = 447.593 + (9.247 * weight in kg) + (3.098 * height in cm) - (4.330 * age in years) [Women]

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Daily Exercise (Daily kCal need): 
Sendentary = BMR x 1.2
Light = BMR x 1.375
Moderate = BMR x 1.55
Heavy  = BMR x 1.725
Extreme = BMR x 1.9

-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Goals:

Maintain Weight
To maintain weight, you might required calories by 500 per day.

Weight Loss
To lose one pound per week, you might decrease your required calories by 500 per day.
(-500 calories X 7 days per week = -3500 calories)

Weight Gain
To gain one pound per week, you might increase your required calories by 500 per day.
(500 calories X 7 days per week = 3500 calories)

Recommended:
A minimum of 1200 calories per day for females.
A minimum of 1800 calories per day for males.
